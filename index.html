What is digital citizenship?

Digital citizenship means using technology in a way that is respectful, safe, and responsible. It includes what you post, how you treat people online, and how you protect your privacy.

Respect Privacy Safety Responsibility Rights Kindness

Why it matters

What we do online can affect real people. Kind and smart choices can make online spaces better for everyone and prevent harm.

Mini checklist

  • Think before you post: would I say this face-to-face?
  • Protect your private info (address, phone, passwords).
  • Be respectful and don’t spread rumors.
  • If you see bullying, report it and support the person.
