Project goal

The goal of this website is to teach the basics of digital citizenship in a simple way. It explains how to be respectful online, protect privacy, and make smarter choices on the internet.

How people benefit

  • Students learn safer and kinder online habits.
  • People understand how small choices can prevent harm.
  • It helps create more positive and respectful online communities.
