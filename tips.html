The 5 Golden Rules

  1. Be respectful: don’t insult, bully, or embarrass others.
  2. Think before posting:because once it’s online, you can’t really take it back
  3. Protect privacy: don’t share personal info or passwords.
  4. Check facts: don’t spread misinformation or rumors.
  5. Help others: support people who are targeted and report harmful content.

Privacy Checklist

  • Use strong passwords (don’t reuse them).
  • Don’t share your address, phone number, or personal details publicly.
  • Review privacy settings on apps.
  • Be careful with location tagging.

Respect Checklist

  • Disagree without being rude.
  • Don’t shame or make fun of people.
  • Don’t post when you’re angry — wait, then respond calmly.
  • Be supportive instead of negative.

Smart Sharing Checklist

  • Ask: “Is this true?” before reposting.
  • Don’t share private screenshots of people.
  • Give credit if you use someone’s work.
  • Keep your accounts secure (2FA if possible).

Quick Reminder

A simple way to remember digital citizenship is: Be kind. Be safe. Be smart.
