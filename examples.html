Example 1: Someone is stressed

Good ✅

Post: “I’m overwhelmed with school.”

Reply: “I’m sorry you’re feeling that. Want to talk? You’ve got this.”

Why it’s good: Kind, supportive, and respectful.

Bad ❌

Post: “I’m overwhelmed with school.”

Reply: “Stop complaining. Everyone has problems.”

Why it’s bad: It puts the person down and can make them feel worse.

Example 2: Sharing private information

Good ✅

Action: You keep your account private and avoid posting your location.

Why it’s good: Protects your safety and privacy.

Bad ❌

Action: You post your full address, phone number, or school schedule.

Why it’s bad: Strangers can use personal info to harm you.

Example 3: Spreading information

Good ✅

Action: You check if something is true before reposting.

Why it’s good: Helps stop rumors and misinformation.

Bad ❌

Action: You repost a rumor without checking.

Why it’s bad: It can harm people and spread false info.

What should you do if you see bullying?

  • Don’t join in or laugh.
  • Support the person being targeted (a kind message helps).
  • Report the behavior on the platform.
  • If needed, tell a trusted adult/teacher.
